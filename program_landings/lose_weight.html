Ready for a fresh start?

Alaska Department of Health is working with HALT (Health and Lifestyle Training) to help Alaskans join the right program for them to lose weight or prevent diabetes.

Find a weight-loss and diabetes prevention program available where you live

Alaska’s health department supports many great options. Want to join an online program focused on losing weight AND preventing diabetes? That’s called a Diabetes Prevention Program (DPP). If you don’t meet the eligibility requirements for the DPP, you’re likely eligible for another option. That’s called a Healthy Lifestyle Program to lose weight and make other healthy changes. We will match you with the best program for you. Participants in online programs track progress and work with assigned coaches using the mobile app or computer.

Click the button below to get started.

Alaska Department of Health is able to support hundreds of Alaskans to join online programs at no cost to them.

Welcome to Alaska Department of Health's Diabetes Prevention and Healthy Lifestyle Programs.

In partnership with the HALT (Health and Lifestyle Training) team, we offer professionally guided coaching programs to help Alaskan residents live healthier lives. The Alaska Department of Health is able to support hundreds of Alaskans to join online programs. Please provide your contact information, and a team member will follow up with program details and your potential start date.

You will be contacted by one of our team members to provide details and complete your registration. We look forward to working with you!

All information you enter on an application form is confidential. It will not be shared with anyone outside the health program, including with State of Alaska staff. After you enroll, Alaska public health staff may receive some information strictly to evaluate and improve the program. None of this information includes details that could be used to identify you or other specific program participants.
